Indoor Air Quality Monitor

The AirQuality Monitor tracks indoor air quality in homes, schools, or workplaces, detecting pollutants from activities like cooking, cleaning, smoking, wood burning, interior decorating, and external sources such as wildfire smoke and traffic emissions. It provides real-time and historical data accessible through the free IQAir AirVisual mobile app.

A cutting-edge outdoor air quality monitor that delivers precise, real-time data on the air quality near your home, school, or workplace.
